Introduction

Tab Manager Pro is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension handles information when you use Tab Manager Pro.

The most important thing to know: Tab Manager Pro does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers. All data remains locally on your device.

Information We Access

When you use Tab Manager Pro, the extension accesses the following information locally on your device:

1. Tab Information

  • What we access: Tab titles, URLs, favicons, and tab states
  • Why we need it: To display and organize your tabs
  • Where it's stored: Only in browser memory while the extension is open
  • Legal basis: Legitimate interest in providing the service

2. Workspace Data

  • What we store: Collections of tab URLs and titles
  • Why we need it: To save and restore tab sessions
  • Where it's stored: Locally in Chrome's storage API
  • Retention: Until you delete them or uninstall

3. User Preferences

  • What we store: Settings and premium license status
  • Why we need it: To remember your preferences
  • Where it's stored: Locally in Chrome's storage API
  • Retention: Until you change them or uninstall

4. Analytics Data (Premium Feature)

  • What we track: Time spent on domains and visit counts
  • Why we need it: To provide productivity insights
  • Where it's stored: Locally in Chrome's storage API
  • Retention: 30 days rolling window

Information We DO NOT Collect

❌ Personal information
❌ Browsing history
❌ External server data
❌ Analytics services
❌ Third-party sharing
❌ Cloud storage
❌ Cookies or tracking
❌ Network requests

Your Rights Under GDPR

As a user based in the European Union, you have the following rights:

1. Right to Access

View all data stored by the extension directly in the interface

2. Right to Rectification

Modify your saved workspaces and preferences at any time

3. Right to Erasure

Delete data through the extension or by uninstalling

4. Right to Data Portability

Export all tabs and workspaces to JSON (Premium)

5. Right to Object

Use the extension without saving any data

6. Right to Lodge a Complaint

Contact CNIL at www.cnil.fr

Children's Privacy

Tab Manager Pro is not directed to children under 16 (the age of digital consent in France). We do not knowingly process information from children under 16.
